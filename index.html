Admissions

Entry Criteria

Standard Entry Pathways

To qualify for the Master of Business Administration, applicants generally need one of the following:

  • A recognised bachelor’s degree in a related discipline (e.g., Business, Management).
  • A recognised bachelor’s degree in any discipline plus 2 years of relevant work experience.
  • A recognised Graduate Certificate in a related discipline.

* English language proficiency requirements apply for international students or non-native speakers.

Recognition of Prior Learning

We value your experience. If you have significant managerial experience but lack a formal degree, you may still be eligible via our Professional Entry Pathway.

  • Assessment based on professional portfolio and CV.
  • Potential credit transfer for previous postgraduate studies.
